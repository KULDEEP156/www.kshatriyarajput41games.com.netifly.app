Get ready to get spooked in Fear Response! In this 3D Horror game, you play as Harry - an agent for the Fear Response Team. It's up to you to investigate the ruins of an old mansion and unravel the mystery that surrounds the former owners. What happened to the happy couple and their baby Cheryl? And what stalks the halls of the manor to this day? Find out as you solve puzzles, creep through the mansions hallways and make your way to the exit. Can you escape the mansion with your life?
